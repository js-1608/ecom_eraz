Common questions about selling on Amazon, Flipkart, and other marketplaces.

Absolutely! Marketplaces like Amazon and Flipkart act as your website. You do not need your own separate website to start selling, though we can build one for you later to build your brand.

Marketplaces offer their own logistics (like Amazon FBA or Flipkart Ekart). They will pick up the product from your location and deliver it to the customer. We help you set this up.

Marketplaces usually have a payment cycle of 7 to 15 days. The payment is transferred directly to your registered bank account after deducting their referral fees.

Returns are part of e-commerce. We train you on how to manage returns effectively, how to file claims for damaged returns (SAFE-T claims), and how to reduce return rates through quality listings.
